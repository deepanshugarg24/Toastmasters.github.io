ABOUT TOASTMASTERS

Toastmasters International is a nonprofit educational organization that teaches public speaking and leadership skills through a worldwide network of clubs. Headquartered in Englewood, Colo., the organization's membership exceeds 358,000 in more than 16,800 clubs in 143 countries. Since 1924, Toastmasters International has helped people from diverse backgrounds become more confident speakers, communicators, and leaders.

The Chitkara University Toastmasters Club has been actively organizing events or weekly colloquios since the time of it's establishment.





UNIVERSITY EVENTS

The University Club has been very active and consistent in organising meetings for the active participation of all it's members. The students work on every aspect of their speaking skills while they are given roles to perform that change evrey week along with the topic of the meeting. The meetings known as the 'Colloquios' among the club members stretch the skills of all the participants in every manner possible.




REGISTRATIONS

The registration to the club is as easy as walking in and paying the entry fees.The registrations are made by the University itself as for the convenience of the students. The students can also apply for scholarship to the club which is also offered by the university to encourage all the brilliant minds!
